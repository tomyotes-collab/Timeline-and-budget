Why timing matters

Consumers rarely convert on first exposure. Research consistently shows that well-sequenced messages across phases maximise both reach and conversion efficiency — so campaigns are planned in three phases.

Budgeting & ROI

Budget follows the phases. Here's a real Vietnamese campaign example totalling about ₫1.17 billion.

Pre 26.5%
Launch 48.4%
Post 12%
Buffer 13%
Pre-launch · ₫309M Launch · ₫565M Post-launch · ₫140M Contingency · ₫152M
Rules of thumb Pre-launch ≈ 20–30%, launch ≈ 40–60% (the biggest slice), post-launch ≈ 20–30%. Always add a contingency of 15–20% for the unexpected. Finally, judge success with ROI — a "hard" business metric of profit relative to cost (Gopinath, 2025).
